You are invited to the Healthy You closed beta. Healthy You is a wellness companion app for testing local wellness tracking, daily summaries, profile/data controls, and a text-based health assistant experience.

Do not use it for emergencies, urgent symptoms, medication decisions, or clinical decisions. Contact a qualified clinician or local emergency service if you need medical help.

Install steps:

1. Download the APK from this approved closed beta link: [APK download link]
2. Confirm the APK came from this message or the approved project owner channel.
3. If you can verify hashes, confirm the APK SHA256 matches `37C22335DEBB025727B518D0617C2670D8ED9F70E2AFA3EBD3737066206F14C8`.
4. Install the APK on your Android device.
5. Open Healthy You.
6. Register a new beta account or log in with an assigned account.
7. If Android asks for Health Connect permission, you may allow or deny it. Denying it should not block testing.

First test checklist:

- Open Home/Data and review Daily Briefing and Weekly Summary.
- Open Nutrition and try local meal or hydration tracking.
- Open Fitness and try workout completion tracking.
- Open Schedule and try habit or medication daily tracking.
- Open Profile and review privacy/data controls.
- Open Export Local Data and confirm the preview opens.
- Open Medibot and send a short text question.
- Close and reopen the app to check that the session remains stable.

Known deferred features:

- Food Scan.
- Voice input.
- File or image attachments.
- Push notifications and reminder delivery.
- Calendar integration.
- Backend account deletion.
- Live OpenAI-backed responses while staging reports OpenAI is not configured.

Please report confusing copy around deferred features, but do not treat the deferred feature itself as a bug unless it crashes, misleads you, or behaves unsafely.

When reporting a bug, include:

- Device model.
- Android version.
- App version: `v0.26.0-alpha`.
- Screen or feature tested.
- Steps to reproduce.
- Expected result.
- Actual result.
- Severity: P0 blocker, P1 major, P2 minor, or P3 feedback.
- Screenshot or short video if safe to share.
- Whether the issue happened once, sometimes, or always.
- Network condition: Wi-Fi, mobile data, offline, or weak connection.

Privacy reminder:

- Do not send passwords, tokens, private medical records, government IDs, financial information, or sensitive screenshots.
- Do not enter urgent symptoms or emergency medical details into the app.
- Do not paste passwords or secrets into Medibot.
